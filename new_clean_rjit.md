Hostel Facilities


RJIT Campus has separate hostel facilities for boys and girls. There are three hostels namely
**APJ Kalam Hostel**, **C. V. Raman Hostel** for boys and **Kalpana Chawla Hostel** for girls.


Each hostel is **120 bedded** and provides a safe, disciplined and comfortable living environment.
Students benefit from structured daily routines, academic focus, physical activities and a healthy hostel culture.


Along with academics, hostel life includes sports, cultural activities, interaction and overall personality development.


##### Facilities

- Separate hostels for boys and girls
- Mess facilities
- Secure and disciplined environment
- Sports and physical activities
- Academic-friendly atmosphere
